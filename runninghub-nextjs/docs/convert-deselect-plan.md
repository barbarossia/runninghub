## Overview
Clear video selections after a successful Convert action in the Convert tab.

## Current State
- Selected videos remain selected after starting conversion.

## Target State
- After conversion starts successfully, selected videos are deselected.

## Requirements
- Only change selection state; keep existing conversion behavior unchanged.
- Apply to the Convert tab flow.

## Implementation Phases
1. Clear video selection after successful conversion start.
2. Manual check in Convert tab.
